1

2

3

4

Tap Add to Home Screen, then tap Add

Don’t see “Add to Home Screen”?
1. Scroll the share list all the way down
2. Tap Edit Actions… (or Edit)
3. Find Add to Home Screen → tap +
4. Tap Done, open Share again — it should appear
